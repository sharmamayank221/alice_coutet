Description

Faith. How much faith can a person have? I wanted to depict faith and the first story that came to my mind was that of Daniel in the lion’s den. Daniel prayed to God even though there was a law at the time indicating that he should only pray to King Darius. The consequence was to be thrown in the lion’s den. Daniel was a friend of King Darius and so King Darius was deeply distressed that Daniel was to be thrown with the lions. Hoping that Daniel was to be delivered, the King put him in the den in accordance with the law. The following morning the King called out to Daniel who responded with “O King, live forever. My God sent His angel and shut the lions’ mouth..”. The king was exceedingly glad, writing a decree that Daniel’s God is the living God.

This painting shows the lion, silent, looking at the angel who has shut it’s mouth. Daniel’s Faith.

I use a variety of styles, colours and techniques in my paintings. I find my inspiration from family life, travelling, world events and life experiences. The beauty in this world that God has created is my muse, whether it be through the human life, nature or experiences.

I aspire to create work that is expressive and emotive at the same time fun, exciting and colourful. I love creating paintings that will bring a message of love, joy, hope and peace to the viewer. I also aspire to inspire viewers to create something for themselves. I can design, draw, paint and create any idea but what I enjoy the most and specialize in is painting. Whilst I was trained to work with oil paints and love working with this medium, my most common medium is acrylic paint because they dry faster and this was an easier medium to use with little kids running around who wanted to touch paintings! My art career has led me to paint abstracts, landscapes, portraits and whatever my client envisions.

My name is Alice, most people call me Alice, but some call me Alissimo, Allie, Ally Pally and Pal and even Alison. I was born and raised in Surrey, England, as one of 5 siblings, from loving Mauritian parents who would tell us about the amazing and beautiful island of Mauritius. Perhaps that’s why I ended up on an island myself.

My husband and I now live in Bermuda with our three amazing children, Tai, Jaime and Cruz. By day I work fulltime at an Insurance Company and by night my creativity is explored! With all the challenges that life brings and the many hats I have to wear, every day is filled with challenges, opportunities and adventures.

Since I was a child I’ve always loved to draw and paint and my hobby has led me to where I am today. I would draw at church, on the bus, at home, and sometimes on the desks at school, until I got found out! Even when I didn’t have the tools to draw I would look at scenes calculating in my mind how and where the lines connect. Although I don’t always have the time to explore every idea floating in my mind, from the time I wake up in the morning to the time I go to sleep I find myself dreaming about the next painting.

hello

When I first came to Bermuda I was mesmerized by Bermuda’s scenery and started to paint in my spare time. I volunteered at Masterworks to help with other artists exhibitions and it gave me the idea to have my own exhibition.

I was hooked after that! Now today, I am a well known and respected artist in Bermuda and I have exhibited at most of the prestigious art galleries in Bermuda. I have entered many competitions and won various awards. People enjoy my artwork all around the world which encourages me to paint more and make my artwork more accessible across the globe.

Living through these changing times has forced me to ask myself what is my vision, my purpose and why has God given me this talent and have I been hiding it for too long. I came to the conclusion that there is no better time than now. I am an artist and always will be. I believe God has given me this talent for a reason and I know I have to use it to further His work. “In all your ways acknowledge Him, And He shall direct your paths”. Proverbs 3:6. My purpose is to utilize my talent to illustrate and capture the beauty of the world He has created for us and to give hope and joy through my paintings.

Thank you for visiting. Your support is much appreciated!
